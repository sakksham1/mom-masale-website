Fruit Chaat

Step-by-step Fruit Chaat recipe made with Mom Masale ingredients for authentic Indian flavour.

⏱ Prep: 10 min 🔥 Cook: 20 min 🍽 Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 cups Seasonal Fruits (Apple, Banana, Papaya, Pomegranate)
  • 1 tsp Mom Masale Chaat Masala 🛒 Shop Chaat Masala
  • 1 tsp Lemon Juice

Method

  1. Combine all chopped fruits in a large bowl.
  2. Add fresh lemon juice.
  3. Sprinkle Mom Masale Chaat Masala.
  4. Toss gently.
  5. Serve immediately.
