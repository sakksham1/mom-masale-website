About Us

Our Story

Sakksham Enterprises was founded with a simple belief — that great food starts with great spices. Under the Mom Masale brand, we manufacture pure, quality Indian spices using traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Based in Shuklaganj, Unnao, we take pride in sourcing the finest raw ingredients and processing them with care to preserve their natural aroma and flavour.

Our Values

  • 100% natural ingredients — no artificial additives
  • Hygienic manufacturing practices
  • Consistent quality in every packet
  • Fair pricing for families and businesses alike

Why Mom Masale?

The name says it all — the warmth and authenticity of a mother's kitchen, in every pinch of spice.
